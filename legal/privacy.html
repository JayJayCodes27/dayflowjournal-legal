Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 4, 2026
Last Updated: January 4, 2026
Key Privacy Commitment: DayflowJournal is designed with privacy at its core. Your journal entries, photos, voice recordings, and all personal data remain entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your journal content on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored exclusively on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Journal Entries: All written, handwritten, and typed journal entries
  • Voice Recordings: Audio recordings created within the app
  • Photos: Images attached to your journal entries
  • Mood Data: Mood ratings, emotions, and day descriptors you record
  • Drawing Data: Handwriting and sketches created with Apple Pencil
  • Settings & Preferences: App theme, font size, notification preferences
  • Smart Prompts: AI-generated prompts created on-device using Apple's NaturalLanguage framework

1.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, access, or transmit:

  • Your journal content
  • Your personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Your location data
  • Your device identifiers
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Third-party tracking cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 On-Device Processing Only

All data processing happens entirely on your device:

  • AI Features: Smart prompts, emotion analysis, and handwriting recognition use Apple's on-device frameworks
  • Insights Generation: Mood trends and writing patterns calculated locally
  • Search: Full-text search processes data on your device only

2.2 Premium Subscription Processing

When you purchase a premium subscription:

  • Payment Processing: Handled entirely by Apple through the App Store
  • Purchase Verification: We verify your subscription using Apple's StoreKit framework (on-device)

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • All data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • Data is protected by iOS's standard file protection mechanisms
  • Biometric lock requires Face ID or Touch ID authentication

3.2 Backups

  • iCloud Backup: If enabled on your device, journal data may be backed up to iCloud (controlled by iOS settings)
  • Manual Export: Premium users can export entries as JSON or text files

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Apple Services We Use

  • StoreKit: For in-app purchases (Apple handles all payment data)
  • LocalAuthentication: For Face ID/Touch ID (biometric data never leaves your device)
  • UserNotifications: For local reminder notifications
  • PhotosPicker: For selecting photos from your library
  • Vision & NaturalLanguage: For on-device AI processing

4.2 No Third-Party Analytics or Advertising

We do not currently use Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, or any advertising networks.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

5.1 Free Tier Users

  • 7-Day Rolling History: Entries older than 7 days are automatically deleted
  • You are notified of this limitation when using the free version

5.2 Premium Users

  • Unlimited History: Keep all journal entries indefinitely
  • Entries remain until manually deleted or app is uninstalled

5.3 Account Deletion

  • No Account Required: DayflowJournal does not require account creation
  • Uninstalling the App: Removes all data from your device permanently

6. Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR (EU Users)

  • Right to Access: Access all your data by exporting your journal
  • Right to Rectification: Edit or correct any journal entry in the app
  • Right to Erasure: Delete individual entries or uninstall the app
  • Right to Data Portability: Export data in JSON format

Under CCPA (California Users)

  • Right to Know: Review all data within the app
  • Right to Delete: Delete entries or uninstall the app
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell your personal information

7. Children's Privacy

We comply with COPPA and similar regulations:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • All data remains on-device, preventing unauthorized collection
  • Parents can review and delete their child's journal data

8. International Data Transfers

Not Applicable: Since all data remains on your device, there are no international data transfers.

9. Contact Information

Questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy? Contact us:

Email: privacy@dayflowjournal.app
Website: https://dayflowjournal.app

Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours.

Our Commitment to Privacy

DayflowJournal was built with one core principle: Your journal is yours alone.

We will never:

  • ❌ Collect your journal content
  • ❌ Use your data for advertising
  • ❌ Sell your information to third parties
  • ❌ Train AI models on your writing
  • ❌ Require account creation or email

We will always:

  • ✅ Process data on-device only
  • ✅ Give you full control over your data
  • ✅ Be transparent about any changes
  • ✅ Minimize data collection
  • ✅ Respect your privacy rights

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified within the app. Continued use constitutes acceptance of updated terms.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of January 4, 2026.
